If your interview answers always sound the same, it's not a willpower problem.

**You've been training on generic question lists.**

Real interviewers ask whether you fit *their* JD — and that needs personalized prep.

**Mirror-View** turns your resume + a real job posting into a tailored interview question set.

**5 minutes from sign-up to your first set.**

## Why personalized questions matter

"Top 100 interview questions" lists lack depth — interviewers care about whether you fit *their* posting.

Same role, same company — a different JD wording shifts the focus.

<Callout type="important">
  Without knowing your resume's weak areas, you'll keep getting stuck in the same spots.
</Callout>

## 5-step guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1 — Sign up (Kakao / Naver OAuth)">
    Open the Mirror-View app.

    Sign in with Kakao or Naver in seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2 — Upload your resume PDF">
    Profile → "Add resume" → pick the PDF.

    Adding a portfolio PDF improves accuracy.

    <Callout type="note">
      Bilingual KO/EN resumes are fine — the AI handles both.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3 — Add the job posting">
    JD menu → "New posting", two options:

    - **Paste the JD text**
    - **Paste the URL** (auto-crawled)

    <Callout type="tip">
      The more detailed the requirements/preferred-skills/responsibilities, the more accurate the matching.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4 — Generate a question set">
    Pick 1 resume + 1 JD + question count (10 / 15 / 20).

    Tap "Generate". Status flips to `PENDING` and back to `COMPLETED` in 1–3 minutes.

    **Watch a rewarded ad to use it for free**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 5 — Review + practice">
    Each question shows:

    - **Category**: technical / experience / behavioral / scenario
    - **Difficulty**: ★ ★ ★
    - **Model answer** (a starting point)
    - **Interview tip** (what to emphasize)

    Practice options:
    - Type answers — feed weakness analysis
    - **Real-time AI mock interview** (voice and video, rolling out progressively)
  </Step>
</Steps>
