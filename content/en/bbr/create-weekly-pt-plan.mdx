If you've ever spent Sunday night thinking "what should I train this week?" — and then defaulted to the same routine, or skipped the week entirely — you're not alone.

The hardest part of consistency isn't motivation. It's **decision fatigue**.

**PocketFit (포켓핏)** is built so the AI handles that decision for you.

**Pick goals, days, and time — your weekly PT plan is ready every Monday.**

## Why automated planning matters

The biggest reason consistent gym-goers fall off isn't willpower; it's the cost of choosing what to do.

Removing that cost lowers the barrier to walking into the gym.

<Callout type="tip" title="A small change that compounds">
  AI applying progressive overload from your past sessions reduces plateaus.
</Callout>

## 5 steps to your first PT plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1 — Install + Kakao sign-in">
    Search "PocketFit" on the App Store / Google Play.

    Sign in with Kakao once and your profile is synced.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2 — Pick goals">
    **Weight loss / strength / endurance** — pick one or more.

    Adjustable later in My Page.

    <Callout type="note">
      Start with 1–2 to stay focused.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3 — Available days + session length">
    Check the days you can train (Mon–Sun) and enter session duration (40 / 50 / 60 / 90 min).

    This drives whether the AI suggests a 2-split, 3-split, or 5-split routine.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4 — Generate the weekly PT plan">
    Tap "Generate PT plan". The Gemini-based engine returns a full week in about 30 seconds.

    Each day shows exercises, sets, and reps. Don't like an exercise? Swap it from the library.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 5 — First session at the gym + logging">
    Open today's plan; weights from past sessions pre-fill.

    Hit "Finish" — duration and calories are auto-tracked.

    <Callout type="tip">
      Next Monday's plan applies **progressive overload** based on your records.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>
