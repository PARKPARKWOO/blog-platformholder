If your interview answers always sound the same, it's not a willpower problem. **You've been training on generic question lists.** Real interviewers ask whether you fit *their* JD — and that needs personalized prep.

Mirror-View turns your resume + a real job posting into a tailored interview question set. **5 minutes from sign-up to your first set.**

## Why personalized questions matter

- "Top 100 interview questions" lists lack depth — interviewers care about whether you fit *their* posting.
- Same role, same company — a different JD wording shifts the focus.
- Without knowing your resume's weak areas, you'll keep getting stuck in the same spots.

## 5-step guide

### Step 1 — Sign up (Kakao / Naver OAuth)

- Open the app or web (`mirror-view.platformholder.site`).
- Sign in with Kakao or Naver in seconds.

### Step 2 — Upload your resume PDF

Profile → "Add resume" → pick the PDF. Adding a portfolio PDF improves accuracy.

> Bilingual KO/EN resumes are fine — the AI handles both.

### Step 3 — Add the job posting

JD menu → "New posting", two options:
- **Paste the JD text**
- **Paste the URL** (auto-crawled)

> The more detailed the requirements/preferred-skills/responsibilities, the more accurate the matching.

### Step 4 — Generate a question set

- Pick 1 resume + 1 JD + question count (10 / 15 / 20).
- Tap "Generate". Status flips to `PENDING` and back to `COMPLETED` in 1–3 minutes.
- **Watch a rewarded ad to use it for free**.

### Step 5 — Review + practice

Each question shows:
- **Category**: technical / experience / behavioral / scenario
- **Difficulty**: ★ ★ ★
- **Model answer** (a starting point)
- **Interview tip** (what to emphasize)

Practice options:
- Type answers — feed weakness analysis
- **Real-time AI mock interview** on web (Whisper STT + WebSocket) — voice and video

## Going further

- **Weakness analysis**: After 5+ submissions, your home dashboard shows top-5 weakness keywords as data — you'll know exactly what to drill.
- **Attendance streak**: One visit a day builds a streak with milestone rewards.
- **Resume review**: Request a review on the same resume for instant section-by-section strengths/weaknesses.

## Wrap-up

The hard part of prep is knowing *what* to practice. Mirror-View gives you that starting point in 5 minutes.
